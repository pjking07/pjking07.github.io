Ben Stein has created a documentary entitled Expelled: No Intelligence Allowed." Below is a long trailer to the documentary - release date is currently April 18.

Don't assume that this documentary will be Christian "propoganda" against Darwinism. It won't be - Ben Stein is Jewish. It will simply show the general public what kind of persecution academics are suffering just for questioning Darwinian thought.
