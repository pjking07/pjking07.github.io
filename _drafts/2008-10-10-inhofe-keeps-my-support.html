Jim Inhofe is the incumbent Republican Senator from Oklahoma.

Typically, voting for him would have been a simple choice. However, the bailout package complicated things for the entire election.

Thankfully, he voted against the $700 billion (+$150 billion more) bailout. Therefore, he gets to keep my vote for this election. (Otherwise, I would have had to look at independent options).

How did your senator vote? Cornyn (R-TX), Hutchison (R-TX), and Coburn (R-OK) all voted in support of the bailout. tsk. tsk.
