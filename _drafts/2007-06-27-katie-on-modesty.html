Katie Carroll (my bride-to-be) has been writing an ongoing series on modesty. Most recently she has written a two-part post on a highly controversial topic - swimsuits.

For the past year, Katie has been seriously following the Lord and seeking His will on the modesty issue. When she concludes the series, I will link back to all the posts. For now, we will look at the swimsuit posts.

  1. "Let's Talk About Swimsuits!"
    In this first post, Katie asks and answers the question: Are modern female swimsuits appropriate to be worn in the presence of men?
  2. "Modest Swimsuits 101"
    In this second post, Katie describes what she has decided to wear for a swimsuit, and her experience with it on a trip to the beach.

I highly recommend these posts to all Christian females - and guys should find them interesting too.

I pray that other girls / women will follow Katie's example in this area.
