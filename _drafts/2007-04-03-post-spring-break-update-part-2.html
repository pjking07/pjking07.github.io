After we spent the end of my Spring Break in Norman, Katie and I made the trip to Waco, where we stayed all of her Spring Break.

That week was overloaded with Wedding errands, life errands, and Star Wars chores...

Let me explain - for those of you who don't know, I have a substantial Star Wars collection. From 8th grade to my freshman year of college, I was actively collecting (read: purchasing) new Star Wars toys. I ended up investing a large amount of money into my (hopefully) resellable collection. I have 160 unopened action figures which hang on my wall in Waco.

And that only covers 100 of them...

I also have ~30 posters (I love posters), ~10 cardboard stand-ups (which are awesome), and a good number of boxed toys. The boxed toys have been stored in my closet for all these years.

I don't have a picture on this computer of the two shelves at the top of my closet - which are entirely full of Star Wars toy boxes.

I also have a lot of open items that have stayed on my bookshelf.

So what was the chore? Well, my parents are finally making me get all my stuff out of my old bedroom in Waco. Katie and I started the task of cataloging, photologing, and packing all of my stuff. You can only imagine how great of a fiancee I have. I was only able to start the cataloging process with her (we filled seven 14-gallon tubs with boxes from my closet). Thankfully, she is willing to work with my mother to finish the project while I am in Africa.
