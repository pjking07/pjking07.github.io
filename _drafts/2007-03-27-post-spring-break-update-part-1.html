The kitchen seemed big enough and the dining area actually allows room for a small table (which is better than some other apartments we looked at). The stairs are cliff style and underneath is open. Therefore we will be able to use that space for storage if need be (which is awesome... especially considering my Star Wars collection). One side of the home has a fire-wall that is completely red brick. Below is a floorplan. The actual townhome we're going to get is a mirror image of the plans.

It's 1100 sq. ft. - which is the biggest apartment we looked at - and the rent is the middle of our preferred range. Katie and I are pretty satisfied so far - now we just have to wait until I move in to see how much we really like it.
